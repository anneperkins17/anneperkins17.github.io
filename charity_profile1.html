Overview

Who: The American Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (ASPCA) is a non-profit organization who's mission is to help displaced and abused animals.

Why: The ASPCA strives to advocate for animal welfare.

How: Through donations, volunteers, and pet adoption ASPCA is able to help many animals in countless communities across the United States.

This charity could be a good match if you:

  • Enjoy working and spending time with animals.
  • Care about animal welfare and advocating for animal rights.
  • Looking to help bridge connections between people and pets for adoption.

Volunteer Opportunities

Some of the jobs volunteers might do at their local SPCA include:

  • Canine Companion - spending time with the shelter's dogs and helping them get adopted.
  • Feline Friend - spending time with the shelter's cats and helping them get adopted.
  • Dog Walker - help walk and exercise the shelter's dogs.
  • Animal Care Attendant - help keep the animals and their living spaces clean.
