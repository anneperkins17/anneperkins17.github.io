Welcome to HelpHub

Helping you help others.

Mission Statement

HelpHub's goal is simple: connecting you with local charities in need of volunteers. We offer a wide range of organizations and detailed profiles to help you find the right volunteer program for you.

Getting started is easy. To view the full list of local charities on HelpHub, click the "Charities List" tab located in the upper left hand corner. From there, more detailed profiles for each charity will be available.
