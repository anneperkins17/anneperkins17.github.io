Overview

Who: Habitat for Humanity is a non for profit global organization focused on providing and improving shelter for countless communities across the United States and around the world.

Why: Habitat's mission is to bring people together to build homes with the vision for everyone to have a decent place to live.

How: Through donations and volunteers Habitat is able to help fund the construction of new buildings and renovations to preexisting homes in need, varying from community to community.

This charity could be a good match if you:

  • Want to become more involved in your community.
  • Are interested in construction/ building homes.
  • Want to fight against poverty and homelessness.

Volunteer Opportunities

Many volunteers for Habitat for Humanity engage in construction work to help build new homes. Volunteers do not need to have any experience in construction or building in order to volunteer.

Other volunteer opportunties include helping staff an event and providing food for volunteers.
