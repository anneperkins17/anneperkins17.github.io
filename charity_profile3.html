Overview

Who: United Way is a privately funded charity who's mission is to help improve community welfare across almost all of US communities and countless countries abroad.

Why: United Way strives to improve the quality of health, education, and economic opportunities for those in the community it serves.

How: Through donations and volunteers United Way is able to help people within their community in various ways.

This charity could be a good match if you:

  • Want to help improve your community.
  • Are looking for a variety of different ways to volunteer.
  • Are passionate about improving the accessible health or education in your community.

Volunteer Opportunities

There is a wide variety of ways to volunteer through United Way. Some of those include:

  • Organizing a food drive.
  • Helping a senior citizen file taxes.
  • Offering tuturing or mentoring.
